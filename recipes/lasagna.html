Lasagna

Description

Super Cheesy. Super Greasy. Yum Yum Nation.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups cubed, cooked chicken meat
  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven: 350 degrees
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    1. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat
    2. stir in onion and garlic
    3. whisk in flour until lightly browned and onion is tender (~2 minutes)
    4. Add chicken broth, milk, and salt
    5. whisk continuously for 1 minute
    6. stir in 2 cups mozzarella and 1/4 cup parmesan until well combined
    7. season with basil, oregano, and black pepper
  3. Add lasagna noodles and cook until al dente (8-10 minutes)
  4. Drain and rinse noodles with cold water
  5. Spread 1/3 of the sauce mixture in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, ricotta, and chicken
  7. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/3 sauce, spinach, 2 cups mozzarella, and 1/2 cup parmesan
  8. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, cheese, and spread the remaining sauce evenly over noodles
  9. Sprinkle with parsley and remaining parmesan
  10. Bake in the oven (~35-40 minutes)
