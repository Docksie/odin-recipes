Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Easy to make with tortillas, tender chicken, and a deliciously cheesy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 3/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 pint sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1/3 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water (optional)
  • 8 (10 in) flour tortillas
  • 1 (12 oz) jar taco sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheatt oven to 350 degrees
  2. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat
  3. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink (~5-7 minutes per side)
  4. Transfer chicken to a cutting board and slice into cubes
  5. Return Chicken to skillet
  6. Add onion, 1 cup cheddar, sour cream, parsley, oregano, and black pepper
  7. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese melts. Low heat to avoid cheese splitting.
  8. Stir in tomato sauce, green pepper, garlic, chili powder, and salt; add water as needed
  9. Spoon chicken mixture into tortillas and roll into enchiladas
  10. Arrange enchiladas, seam-side-down, in a baking dish
  11. Top with taco sauce and remaining 3/4 cheddar
  12. Bake in oven uncovered (~20 minutes)
